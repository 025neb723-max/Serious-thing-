About SXC2F

St. Xavier's College Financial Forum (SXC2F) is a premier student-led finance club dedicated to bridging the gap between academic theory and real-world financial practice. Founded in 2018, we have grown into one of the most active and prestigious clubs at St. Xavier's College, Kolkata.

Our forum serves as a dynamic platform where finance enthusiasts come together to learn, share, and grow. Through a blend of seminars, workshops, competitions, and networking events, we equip our members with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the financial world.

Join Our Community

Our Mission

To cultivate a community of financially literate, analytically sharp, and professionally networked students who are prepared to lead in the evolving landscape of global finance.

Founded 2018

Our Founding Story

SXC2F was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate finance students who recognized the need for a dedicated platform to connect academic learning with real-world financial practice. What started as a small study group has blossomed into one of the most vibrant student organizations at St. Xavier's College.
